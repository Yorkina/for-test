When does the project start?
The game starts on October 3rd and will go on for 4 weeks.
Is it possible for people with full-time job to participate in the project?
You can do it even if you have a job, studies, husband, three kids and a dog (you can look for books on time management on Amazon). This training doesn't require any special equipment and can be done at home. Each training session takes from 30 to 40 minutes.
What if I don't have the opportunity to do the workouts every day?
The main rule of the game is to do the kick-off Saturday task. And you get points for every daily and extra tasks done, that might be helpful to go through the game. Anyway, the reason you came here was to gain beautiful body and healthy lifestyle, right? That's why I recommend you to do all the tasks. Your body will be grateful!
How long one workout session lasts?
The average time of one training session from is 30 to 40 minutes.
Until what time I'm supposed to send my daily reports?
Until midnight.
Do the tasks differ for men and for women?
We are welcoming the equal rights of genders. That's why both men and women can participate in the project! Men as well as women!! And the program won't differ.
— Can I try it out?
— Nope
— Can I first try, than pay?
— Niht
— But I doubt myself. What if I suddenly fail.
— No
What can be the reasons to be kicked out from the project?
Incorrect behavior towards other participants or towards the organizers, non-complience with the rules of the project, failure of the kick-off task and falling into negative balans. You are a member of the Fatkiller Club as long as you have possitive balance.
Is it necessary to shoot all the workouts on video?
Only control task should be shot on video.
What if nobody can shoot my workout session?
You have to find the way - invite friends, teach your kids or cat to hold the camera, get the tripod or something else.
But how will you know that I fulfilled the task correctly?
The control of daily workouts will be done in participant's personal account where written report should be published. The control of special tasks will be done by video reports that should be sent to a personal accout for checking. All the reports will be supervised by project's administrators.
I'm embarassed to share a poto of me and my training video in social networks. Is it an obligatory condition for taking part in the game?
Absolutely not obligatory. But you get points for sharing with FKC hashtag. And it is also nice to get additional support from your friends.
Will everybody see my knockout tasks?
Nope, only our Supervisors will. But they are hard to suprise.
How is the winner determined?
Maximum score and most impressive 'before and after" results will bring you to the top of the leaderboard.The voting is done by all the participants that went into the final and sent Before&After photoes. All the participants vote.
What is the scoring system intended for?
The points you get show your progress and you can compare your achievements with other fatkillers.This measurable results connected directly with your body progress and will make it easy for you and your supervisers to assess your motivation.
Why does the game last for 4 weeks?
As a rule, you need 21 day to set up a new habit. We guide and support you throuh these 21 days and then you get an extra week to strenghen your new habit of daily exercises.
What if I have a small child and can't leave the house?
All the tasks are supposed to be done at home. So you don't need to leave your house.
Will the jogging be one of the tasks?
I can't leave the baby. Only knockout task is obligatory. That's why you can skip jogging. But running is a very effective fatburning instrument. And you've come here to get the result!
And what shall do if I have my cycle during the Fatkiller game?
Then you can sympathize yourself. And we will sympathize you too. But only the first day of your cycle! After that the work should be done to achieve the result! If the first day of the cycle falls on Saturday then it is possible to send the knockout task untill the noon on Sunday.
If I have some health restrictions can I still fit to your game?
The project implies big amount of phisical work (squat exercises, jumps, run, push-ups and s.o.) So in order to take a decision about taking part in the game you should consult your physician by showing him the examples of exercises).
And do you prescribe the nutrition plan?
We give our recommendations on nutrishion plan and calculate your individual daily norm of calories as well the balans between proteins, fats and carbohydrates. So you can either calculate your individual nutrition plan by yourself or to follow our recommendations on standart nutrition.
